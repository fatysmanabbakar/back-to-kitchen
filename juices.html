Pineapple juice

Roast the fresh pineapple until it is well cooked on a gas or on a grill.

  • Peel it and cut into small size cubes.
  • Take out the juice of the pineapple.
  • Add all ingredients to it.
  • Squeeze-in the lemon.
  • Serve chilled garnished with roasted cumin and chopped mint.

peer juice

Peel the fruits and cut into small pieces.

  • Chill for 1 hour.
  • Take a powerful blender add crushed ice and water.
  • Drop all the fruits in the blender and blend at high speed for 1 minute.
  • Pour into tall glasses and garnish with sprouts.
Grape Nectar

Muddle the lychees along with the lemon chunks.

  • Make about 120 ml of juice with the black grapes, but don't throw away the pulp
  • In an old fashioned glass, add the muddled fruits, and sugar syrup and crushed ice
  • You can use a shaker to mix them well. Top it up with the fresh grape juice
lychee and dill juice

Peel and deseed the lychees. Place them in a blender along with the remaining ingredients and blend for a few seconds

  • Strain into a glass, top up with ice cubes and garnish with a sprig of dill
Orange and Basil juice

Plum-ness

Take the plums in a bowl.

  • Wash them together thoroughly
  • Now put them in a grinder
  • Add condensed milk and ice along with it to the grinder.
  • Grind the plums to get a refreshing plum smoothie.
  • Pour it into a glass or jug carefully through a sieve.
  • Serve chilled with crushed ice on top.
